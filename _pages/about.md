 I am a graduate student at the Institute for Analytical Sociology, where I study a master’s in Computational Social Science. Before, I earned a B.A. in Sociology from the University of Salamanca.
As a computational sociologist, I am interested in using novel methods to tackle sociological questions such as the emergence, enforcement, and break down of social norms. I have also studied how Digitization and streaming have affected the consumption of popular music across countries. You can read more about this and other stuff I have done in my portfolio.
