I am a graduate student at the Institute for Analytical Sociology, where I study a master's in [Computational Social Science](https://liu.se/en/education/program/f7mcd). Before, I earned a B.A. in sociology from the University of Salamanca. 

As a computational sociologist, I am interested in using novel methods to tackle some of the core sociological questions such as the emergence and enforcement of social norms and conventions or the ways in which culture diffuses. I have also worked in studying how music consumption has been affected at a global scale by digitization and the advent of streaming. You can read more about this and other stuff I have done on the projects section.
