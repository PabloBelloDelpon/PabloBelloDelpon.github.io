I am a graduate student at the Institute for Analytical Sociology, where I study a master's in [Computational Social Science](https://liu.se/en/education/program/f7mcd). Before, I earned a B.A. in sociology from the University of Salamanca. 

As a computational sociologist, I am interested in using novel methods to tackle some sociological questions such as the emergence and enforcement of social norms and conventions or how culture diffuses. I have also worked on studying how Digitization and streaming have affected the consumption of popular music across countries. You can read more about this and other stuff I have done in my portfolio.
